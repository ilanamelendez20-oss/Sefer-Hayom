Today's zmanim
Sof Zman Shema is shown by both the Gra and the Magen Avraham (earlier) opinions — say the morning Shema before the earlier time when you can. Candle-lighting is 18 minutes before sunset. For Sephardic practice (Edot HaMizrach / following Rav Ovadia Yosef), nightfall and the end of Shabbat may use a different shiur than shown — confirm tzeit and Motzaei Shabbat with your luach.
